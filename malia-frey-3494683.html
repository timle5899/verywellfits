Experience

Malia Frey has been in the health and fitness industry for over 25 years as an educator, health coach, writer, and editor. She started writing for Verywell Fit in 2016 and has since produced a large body of evidence-based health, wellness, fitness, diet, nutrition, and exercise content for the brand. She also works with physicians, registered dietitians, behavioral health specialists, and fitness professionals to develop web content, instructional materials, and news articles that guide dieters through the process of setting and reaching their weight loss goals.

Malia is an American Council on Exercise (ACE) certified health coach and personal trainer with additional ACE credentials in fitness nutrition, weight management, behavior change, and functional aging. She also is a graduate of the National Institutes of Health Medicine in the Media program.
