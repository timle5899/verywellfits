Experience

Melissa Rifkin is a Verywell Fit medical reviewer and a Connecticut-based registered dietitian with over 15 years of experience working in the clinical setting with a large patient base focused on weight management, bariatric nutrition, and oncology nutrition.

In 2021, Melissa worked with a pediatric gastroenterologist and speech pathologist to write "Confession of a Dietitian, Mama & Max Take on the First Year of Solids," using science-based information to educate parents on the stages of food introduction, food sensitivities and allergies, and eating challenges. Melissa holds a Bachelor of Science in Nutritional Science from The University of Connecticut and a Master of Science in Clinical Nutrition from Steinhardt, New York University.
