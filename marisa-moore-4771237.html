Verywell Fit Wellness Reviewer, Registered Dietitian-Nutritionist

Expertise

Nutrition, Dietetics

Education

Georgia State University, Georgia Institute of Technology

Highlights

  • At Verywell Fit: Marisa is a registered dietitian-nutritionist who has been a member of the Verywell Fit review board since 2019. She uses her nutrition and culinary expertise, along with science-based research, to review nutrition, dietary, health, and weight management content to ensure accuracy for the brand.
  • Certifications: Registered dietitian-nutritionist; licensed dietitian
  • U.S. region: Southeast
  • Residential area: Suburban

Healthy living is about flexibility, balance, and discovering what works for your body. As a registered dietitian nutritionist, my mission is to empower women to find their personal path to health without rules and restrictions. I focus on a minimally processed, plant-forward way of eating, but nothing is off limits—including bread, butter, or whatever you’re thinking of right now. I don’t believe in guilty pleasures when it comes to food. Let’s instead focus on enjoying it!

— Marisa Moore, RDN, MBA

Experience

Marisa is a nationally-recognized registered dietitian-nutritionist, culinary nutrition expert, and plant-forward cookbook author who has been serving as a review board member for Verywell Fit since 2019. She has worked on numerous articles, reviewing nutrition, dietary, health, and weight management content to ensure accuracy.

She is also the founder of Marisa Moore Nutrition, where she partners with well-aligned brands to promote health and wellness to the public through her writing, social media content, and consulting. Marisa received her bachelor’s degree in nutrition science and MBA in marketing from Georgia State University.

About Verywell Fit

Verywell Fit, a People Inc. Brand, nutrition and fitness destination that provides science-backed information and tools to empower people to take realistic steps towards a healthier life. We reach more than 150 million readers annually. Verywell Fit content is fact checked and reviewed by our Review Board for accuracy and integrity. Learn more about us and our editorial process.
